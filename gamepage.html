You are in Newcastle castle. The knights are seated at the round table and are waiting for your magnificient speech.

Press the thumbs up to enter the main hall

You enter the hall to see a crowd of knights, ladies and lords. The king of England has issued a decree to rescue the kingdom from the hands of the ememy. He needs every warrior to defend their city and town.

Do you eloquently explain that bravery is overated and the king is overeracting, claiming he is merely drunk and delusional.
or... Do you majestically sound the trumpet and exhort your knights to defend Newcastle at all costs?
