Welcome Valiant Knight of Newcastle!

You have the task of gathering the knights and saving the king from the rebellion.

Through a series of twists and turns, you have to collect the weapons and defend the kingdom at all costs. But be warned, betrayal is never far away, trusting the wrong person could prove fatal...


The main page is above the buttons and tells you the storyline and plot of the game so far.


The boxes will give you two options to choose from. Press one and see how you fair, O bravest soul..

The castle button starts/resets the game to the beginning
